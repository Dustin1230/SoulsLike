Mouse + Keyboard:

WASD: Move
Mouse: Look around
Left Click: Attack
Space: Dodge
Shift (Held): Sprint
F: Find Target
Tab: Level Up
Q: Search Left
E: Search Right

Controller:

Left Stick: Move
Right Stick: Look around
Left Face Button: Attack
Right Face Button: Dodge
Left Stick Click (Held): Sprint
Right stick clicl: Find Target
Left button special: level up
(while locked on) Right Stick left: Search left
(while locked on) Right Stick right: Search right
